Barry King's Impact

Barry King has dedicated his career to coaching elite-level juniors and adults in tennis. Over his 39 years of professional coaching, Barry has been the Head Coach and Director at the Prince Academy and a USTA/RTC Regional Training Center. His commitment to excellence and fostering talent has earned him numerous accolades, including the 2009 USTA Coach of the Year.

Throughout his coaching career, Barry has trained more than 250 juniors who went on to receive NCAA tennis scholarships. His students have won over 100 high school singles and doubles championships, and 17 have been named NCAA All-Americans.
